公司简介 Company Introduction

Shaoxing Xunchi
Textiles Co., Ltd

Shaoxing Xunchi Textiles Co., Ltd is a home textiles manufacturer founded in 2010, specialized in various knit fabrics and blankets (fleece, polar fleece, coral fleece, flannel, picnic, and double-sided blankets), cushions, bedding, and other home textile products.

Also, our products have OEKO-TEX Standard 100. The factory located in Shaoxing City, Zhejiang Province, China, which is near Ningbo Port and Shanghai Port. Our factory is audited by BSCI. With more than 14 years of development, we have our own fabric weaving workshop, dyeing workshop and ready-made workshop. Warmly welcome you to audit our factory.

绍兴迅驰纺织品有限公司是一家成立于2010年的家纺制造商，专注于各种针织面料、多种毯子（例如双面绒、摇粒绒、珊瑚绒、法兰绒、野餐垫等）、件套和其他相关家纺产品。

产品通过 OEKO-TEX STANDARD 100 认证。工厂坐落于中国浙江绍兴，靠近宁波港和上海港。我们所有的产品都遵照国际质量标准，深受全球众多不同市场的喜爱。我们工厂也通过了 BSCI 验厂认证。经过十四年的发展，我们有自己的坯布、染色和成品制作车间，欢迎大家前来参观。
